About Clotho Cares

Clotho Cares is a platform dedicated to bridging the gap between clothing donors and verified NGOs. We strive to reduce textile waste, support underprivileged communities, and promote sustainable fashion choices.

  • Donors can register and track their contributions.
  • NGOs receive sorted clothes based on condition, category, and need.
  • Built for social impact with transparency and accessibility in mind.
